About Us

Shreee Institute was established from the home of Kamini maam as she was passionate about teaching. From humble beginning of 2 kids within few years she graduated to taking a batch of 5. From there withing a decade we reached to class of 20 and today we are proud to teach a large number of students of Gujarat Board from well over 15 different schools in our area.

We started with only SS English and started the Maths Science chapter when Kushan Sir joined the institute 8 years ago. He was able to use his international exposure to bring in projectors and smart boards and eventually the app which has well over 2000 self made videos which helps hundreds of students to improve their result.

We belive in three things when we teach.

EDUCATE - Teaching textbook knowledge is not sufficient. we try to educate our children as to why those textbooks were written, how to apply good things from tetxtbook to our life and how to practically imbibe the academic learning to real life.

ENGAGE - Teaching is interesting and fun for us, but ask a student and he/she would say that studies are a burden. To change the mindset of students we aim to engage them into fun activities, games, quizes and try to provide a more wider approach to study then just textbook learning.
