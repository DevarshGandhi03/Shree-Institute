Welcome to Shree Institute

Shree Institute was established from the home of Kamini maam as she was passionate about teaching. From humble beginning of 2 kids within few years she graduated to taking a batch of 5. From there withing a decade we reached to class of 20 and today we are proud to teach a large number of students of Gujarat Board from well over 15 different schools in our area.
